Firselp Documentation

Building First Aid For Everyone, Everywhere

📌 Project Overview

Firselp is a mobile-first app that provides first aid guidance using AR (Augmented Reality), AI, and multimedia formats. Only 1% of people know how to deliver first aid properly — Firselp aims to change that.

🧠 Problem Statement

  • Traditional training is boring, difficult, and ineffective under stress
  • Thousands of lives could be saved with better knowledge
  • 1% of the population knows first aid — mostly professionals

🎯 Our Solution

Firselp is designed for:

  • 🚨 Emergency and training modes
  • 🧠 Smart AI and multimedia step-by-step guidance
  • 🌍 Accessible anytime, even offline
  • 🎮 Interactive AR for immersive learning
